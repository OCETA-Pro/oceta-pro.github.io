
# About: Open Cutting Edge Tech Academia - OCETA Connect 2026
**Advancing AI & CET-NM in Higher Education, Research, and Industry**

## 1. The Event Scope & Thematic Focus
OCETA Connect 2026 is an exclusive, invite-only academic and industry gathering focused on the practical application of Artificial Intelligence (AI), Extended Reality (XR), and Cutting-Edge Technologies / New Media (CET-NM). We view these technologies not as ends in themselves, but as powerful tools to transform higher education methodologies, streamline research data analysis, and establish dynamic bridges between academia and enterprise sectors.

## 2. Our Core Philosophy
The overarching ethos of the conference is to gather researchers, academics, and industry specialists "under an academic umbrella". This initiative deliberately dismantles traditional academic silos by flattening the hierarchy. We provide a collaborative platform where tenured faculty, industry innovators, and supervised university students (BSc/MSc) interact as peers to solve real-world technical and pedagogical challenges.

## 3. The Principle of "Flexible Openness"
We recognize that modern innovation involves proprietary industry data, pre-patent research, and early-stage academic ideas. OCETA Connect operates on a strict policy of "Flexible Openness". Presenters retain absolute autonomy over their intellectual property, utilizing the platform as a secure, high-trust incubator to present pre-competitive work without compromising institutional or commercial security.

## 4. The Immersive Hybrid Format
OCETA Connect pioneers a borderless conference methodology. The event is hosted simultaneously in a fully immersive spatial computing environment (The VRChat "OCETA Connect Arena") and via a standard enterprise 2D broadcast (Microsoft Teams), ensuring equitable, global access regardless of hardware limitations.

## 5. Organizing Institutions
This project is driven by the interdisciplinary synergy between:
*   **The Faculty of Physics and Astronomy, Adam Mickiewicz University (Poznań, Poland):** Providing the deep technical core and architectural leadership.
*   **The School of Management, HES-SO Valais-Wallis (Switzerland):** Providing the strategic application, outreach, and digital marketing layer.
*   **Contributing Partners:** South East Technological University (SETU, Carlow, Ireland) and California State University, Bakersfield (USA).
