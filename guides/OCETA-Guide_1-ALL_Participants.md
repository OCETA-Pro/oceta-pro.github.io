## Good to know guide

* **Be Patient & Understanding:**  We're pioneering a complex setup, blending VRChat immersion with MS Teams accessibility. While the "OCETA Arena" in VRChat may appear visually simple, the underlying technology is intricate. As with any cutting-edge endeavor, there's a chance of encountering technical hiccups or unexpected script behaviors, especially related to multi-user interactions in VRChat and audio engineering. Your patience is greatly appreciated!
* Presentations are to be shared on MS Teams and streamed into VRChat via Youtube Live streaming.
* **Streaming Delay Notice:**  Please be aware there is an inherent delay of 10–15 seconds in what you see and hear if you are watching the stream inside VRChat. During periods of heavy network load, this delay might extend slightly. Please factor this buffer into all live Q&A interactions and when waiting for presenter responses.
* **"Speak Signals" - IMPORTANT for Q&A/Discussion:**<br>
  * To manage audio and prevent echoing or participants speaking over each other (especially with the stream delay), our **Communication Manager (CM)**  will display visual "Speak Signals" above the main presentation screen (visible in both VRChat and on the MS Teams stream).
  * **Signals will indicate:**
    * **"VRChat Speak" (on a green background)** :
      * **VRChat Attendees:**  If you wish to ask a question or make a comment, please move your avatar to approach one of the **red lines** located in front of the seating rows in the "OCETA Connect Arena." This will activate a "loud speaking mode" so your voice can be heard throughout the Arena and on the stream. Please speak clearly, then step away from the white/red lines. (See OCETA Arena Map [OCETA Arena Map](https://oceta-pro.github.io/guides/OCETA_Arena-Map.html))
    * **"MS Teams Speak" (on a green background)** :
      * Attendees in MS Teams may unmute microphones. **Please pay close attention to these signals and** **mute your microphone when not indicated to speak.**  This is crucial for a smooth audio experience for everyone.

* **Backup Plan - Major Technical Issues:**<br>
  * We have many technical variables in play. If a major technical problem occurs that cannot be resolved quickly, **participants will be directed to switch to the OCETA MS Teams meeting** to continue the event.**  The MS Teams link will be re-shared via all available channels.
* **Individual Technical Difficulties:**<br>
  * If you experience individual technical issues (e.g., your VRChat client crashes, internet connection drops, or VR Chat presentation screen not working etc.), please try to rejoin if possible. If you cannot resolve your VR problems please switch to the OCETA MS Teams meeting

---

* **Presenter’s Issues:**  If a presenter is lost due to technical issues, we will proceed with the next presenter to maintain the schedule. The affected presenter may be able to resume their presentation after the current one finishes, if time permits and their issue is resolved.
* If you are presenting in VR Chat and you hear yourself in the presentation stream please signal the issue to OCETA Operators to turn off returning audio channel for the OCETA Arena.
* **Power Management (Crucial for Standalone VR Headset Users):**<br>
  * Standalone virtual reality devices (such as Meta Quest) experience rapid battery depletion during high-density multi-user events. Ensure your headset, controllers, and any secondary battery packs are 100% charged before entering the instance.
  * Keep a charging cable or external power bank attached during the session. If your device battery drops below critical levels, immediately drop out of VRChat and switch to the backup MS Teams meeting stream to continue watching without interruption.
* **VR Headset Troubleshooting**  - in a case of critical problems like e.g. low battery, no streaming audio or video please join the OCETA MS Team meeting.

Thank you for being part of the OCETA Experience! Your participation and understanding help us shape the future of OCETA Connect.
