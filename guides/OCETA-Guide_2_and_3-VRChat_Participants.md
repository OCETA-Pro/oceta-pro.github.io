## Presenting in VRChat Arena

* **VRchat Indirect Slide Control Panel:**<br>
  * Within the presenter area in VRChat, you will see virtual "buttons."
  * **Use these buttons to signal to the Slides Operator (SO) to manage slides:**
    * Next Slide
    * Previous Slide
    * Jump +5 Slides
    * Jump +5 Slides
    * Jump -5 Slides
    * First Slide
    * Last Slide
  * When you press a button, it will light up, indicating your request is active.
  * The light will go off once the SO has performed the action, confirming it's done.
  * Please wait for this confirmation before you will click another slide's control button. Remember that the slide change will appear on the streaming screen with the 10-15 seconds delay.
* **Audio & Speaking:**<br>
  * Ensure your VRChat microphone is working clearly. Test it before the event.
  * Speak clearly and at a moderate pace, mindful of the streaming delay.
  * Pay attention to the "Speak Signals" displayed above the streaming screen, especially during Q&A.
* **Technical Setup:**<br>
  * Join the "OCETA Arena" in VRChat at least **15-20 minutes before the event starts** for a final tech check and orientation.
  * Ensure your PC/VR equipment is fully set and charged.
* **Q&A:**<br>
  * Questions from the audience on MS Teams Chat will be visible on the main screen.
* **If Technical Issues Occur:**<br>
  * Stay calm. The OCETA Lead will assist. If you lose connection, try to rejoin. If major issues prevent you from presenting in VRChat, we may ask you to switch to presenting via MS Teams (so as a backup plan, please have your slides prepared for
MS Teams presentation in case of problems in VRChat) if feasible, or reschedule your slot if time permits.

We appreciate your contribution to the OCETA Experience!
